## Description

A short exploration of the Madrigal genre. From the pioneers like Philippe Verdelot, to the master like Cipriano de Rore to the Giaches de Wert and Claudio Monteverdi geniuses which brought the renaissance madrigal to its highest expression.

Madrigal for 4 voices (SATB) and 5 voices (SSATB)
